Day 17 — Data Interpretation (DI)

Many students think DI is a new topic.

It is not.

DI = Applying old topics quickly.

Questions mostly use:

Percentages
Ratios
Averages
Profit & Loss
Simple arithmetic

So if your arithmetic is strong, DI becomes easy.

What Companies Test in DI

Companies are NOT testing graph reading.

They are testing:

Speed
Accuracy
Percentage calculations
Approximation skills
Types of DI
1. Table DI

Example:

Year	Sales
2021	100
2022	150
2023	200
2. Bar Graph

Compare values.

3. Pie Chart

Percentage distribution.

4. Line Graph

Growth over time.

5. Caselet DI

Paragraph with data.

No graph.

DI Strategy

Always solve in this order:

Step 1

Read question first.

Step 2

Locate data.

Step 3

Calculate only required values.

Many students read the whole graph first.

Waste of time.
