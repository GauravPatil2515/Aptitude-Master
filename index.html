60-Day Speedrun Strategy & ROI Rules

The market placement strategy in 2026 focuses on becoming very strong at top interview patterns rather than solving 500+ random questions. This curated sheet contains 110 of the most critical coding questions.

Recommended Daily Plan (4 Hours)

  • Hour 1 (Theory): Watch video tutorials (NeetCode/Striver) & take pattern notes.
  • Hour 2-3 (Solve): Work on 3-4 problems. Spend 20 min trying before learning the pattern.
  • Hour 4 (Revision): Write down pattern, time/space complexity, and common tricks.

Placement Strategy & ROI Rules

  • Target: Comfortably clear OAs for TCS, Infosys, Accenture, Cognizant, Wipro, and startups.
  • What to Skip: Segment Trees, Fenwick Trees, Heavy DP/Graphs, Competitive Programming tricks.
  • Balance: 50% DSA (110 Qs) + 25% Core CS (DBMS/OS/CN/OOP/SQL) + 25% Project + Resume.
